It’s never too late to start your journey in tech, all it takes is a step in the right direction

Fellowship

Our fellowship program helps disadvantaged but smart kids get access to best training facility, faculty and mentorship to kickstart their software development career. Our fellowship is a three month long program and we open application three times in a year. This programme is free and successful graduate gets job placement. 100% of our last cohort are employed. Our selection process is rigorous and ONLY about 5% make it to the final slot. However, It’s never too late to start a journey in tech and all it takes is a step. Let’s help us SWITCH

What to Expect

Market driven curriculum

Our curricula are developed with leading technology companies home and abroad willing to hire for skills. We have tailored our content to current and future market need. You are sure guaranteed you will complete our courses with market-ready skills

Practical Training session

Our faculties are top in the industry and are hands-on in their approach to training. You spend time not ONLY learning the theories but also the practical step by steps of how things are built

Soft-skill Development

Do you know that a lot of people don't get hired or advance in their career because of lack of soft skills? You will learn business communication, skill, teamwork, sound work ethics etc

Work on real live project

While you study, you will be made to work on live projects to have a feel of how the real world works. This prepares you for the workplace
